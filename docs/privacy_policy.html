Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

At Save Button, we believe your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy and explains how we handle information within the app.

1. Personal Information

Save Button does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or link your social media profiles to use the app. We do not use trackers or sell your data to third parties.

2. Bookmarks and User Content

The primary function of Save Button is to help you organize links.

  • Optional Storage: If you choose to use the Server feature, the app will store the bookmarks you provide on our servers or a self-hosted server of your option.
  • Control: This feature is entirely optional. You can use the app without backing up data.
  • Data Location: Depending on your device settings, these bookmarks are stored locally on your device or locally and backed up to our servers.

3. Permissions

The app may request basic permissions (such as "Share Sheet" access) solely to allow you to send links to the app. We do not use these permissions to access your photos, contacts, or location.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically for any changes.

Summary: Your privacy is built into the core of Save Button. We save your links only if you ask us to, and we never know who you are.
